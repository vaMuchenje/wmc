ALISON COSTELLO

SVP, Director of Client Services

Alison continually impresses clients with her tireless enthusiasm, and insights into their businesses. She juggles everything. Forgets nothing. And has a gift for pulling together all the right resources for every project. Clients become very attached to her, and why wouldn't they.

Alison began her career at Bain & Co., the strategic management consulting firm. Then, as Director of Partnerships at interactive marketing agency HookMedia, she integrated online and offline marketing programs for clients including Blue Cross and Blue Shield, Oxygen Media, and Domainia.com. That's where we met her, pitching a new client together, and decided we'd hire her someday. Lucky day for us and lucky for our clients.

In addition to also managing her husband and children, Alison makes time to support pro bono causes, including The Support Committee for Battered Women and Tailored for Success.
