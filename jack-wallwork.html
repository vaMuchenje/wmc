Jack Wallwork

CEO/Creative Director

Jack’s work has won more than 250 national awards from the most respected creative competitions: The One Show, The Advertising Club of New York (ANDY), Communication Arts, the Art Directors Club of New York, and the CLIO Awards. Advertising Age has recognized his creative work in both “The 100 Best Television Commercials of the Year” and “The Best Print Ads of the Year". He’s also received more than 70 awards from New England’s Hatch Awards, the largest regional competition in the country.

A writer, creative director and relentless brand fanatic, he may be more passionate about your brand than you are. His notable campaigns include Steinway & Sons, Titleist, John Hancock, Bertucci's, Digital Federal Credit Union (DCU), Wang, Parker Brothers, Blue Cross & Blue Shield, The Boston Globe, Domain, Minuteman Health and Boston Private Bank. In a previous life, Jack was Associate Creative Director at Boston's largest agencies: Hill Holliday Connors Cosmopulos and Humphrey Browning MacDougall.

Jack has served on the board of directors of MADD, The Winchester Foundation for Educational Excellence and the Winchester Scholarship Foundation.

A graduate of Tufts University, Jack’s non-advertising passion is golf. Despite an overbooked schedule, he's always available to play.
