Richard (Rick) McKenna

President

Rick's unique marketing perspective is the result of valuable experience on both the agency and client sides of the business, both here in the U.S. and internationally. His varied client experience includes Boston Scientific, Steinway & Sons, Monster.com, Hatteras Yachts, Solidworks, The Boston Company, Royal Air Maroc and the Residences at the Westin.

Rick serves on several Boards of Directors including Save the Harbor/Save the Bay, British American Business Council, the business advisory board for Zoo New England and the Friends of Winter Pond. Rick has an MS in Public & International Relations from Boston University’s College of Communication and a BA in Classical Philosophy from Boston College. In 2012 Rick was selected as a delegate by the British Consul General in Boston to represent his firm and the New England business area on two trade missions to the UK.

Rick and his wife live in Winchester, and spend their precious free time aboard their boat “Legacy” or at their cottage on Long Island Sound.
